DreamSwapV: Mask-guided Subject Swapping for Any Customized Video Editing
Weitao Wang , Zichen Wang , Hongdeng Shen , Yulei Lu , Xirui Fan , Suhui Wu , Jun Zhang^ , Haoqian Wang^ , Hao Zhang`
^ Corresponding authors ` Project leader

Arxiv Preprint, 2025

We present DreamSwapV, a mask-guided, end-to-end framework for generic video subject swapping with a user-specified mask and reference image. Leveraging our novel condition fusion module and adaptive mask strategy, DreamSwapV achieves high-fidelity results, outperforming prior methods on VBench and our new DreamSwapV-Benchmark.

MVReward: Better Aligning and Evaluating Multi-View Diffusion Models with Human Preferences
Weitao Wang* , Haoran Xu* , Yuxiao Yang , Zhifang Liu , Jun Meng^ , Haoqian Wang^
* Equal contribution ^ Corresponding authors

The Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI), 2025

We propose a comprehensive framework for evaluating multi-view diffusion models and aligning them with human preferences. We introduce MVReward, a reward model trained on 16k expert pairwise comparisons, enabling fair evaluation of image-driven 3D methods, and MVP, a tuning strategy that further improves alignment. Experiments show MVReward is a reliable metric and MVP consistently enhances model performance.

Make Your MoVe: Make Your 3D Contents by Adapting Multi-View Diffusion Models to External Editing
Weitao Wang , Haoran Xu , Jun Meng^ , Haoqian Wang^
^ Corresponding authors

Arxiv Preprint, 2025

We introduce Make Your MoVe (MyMV), a tuning-free, plug-and-play scheme for 3D content editing that preserves geometry while editing appearance. MyMV employs a geometry preservation module with an injection switcher to align edited assets with original normals in a single inference run. Experiments demonstrate consistent improvements in multi-view and mesh quality across diverse models and editing methods.
